National Public School Panjipara

National Public School Panjipara under A Unit of K.M. Chishty Memorial Welfare Society. It is the most trusted name in the field of quality education which is recognised throughout the academic world for its progressive approach and commitment to excellence. The school provides ample opportunities for the holistic and all-round development of the child along with promoting critical thinking. National Public School Panjipara management team is a unique combination of experienced educationist, qualified and trained teachers and corporate manager, it strives to achieve the objectives of the organizations via continuous research, introduction of latest training methodologies and follow up to ensure adherence to set guidelines. Vision: Provide student-centered educational programs that challenge all students to perform at their highest potential to serve the nation. It is a community where all children feel loved, respected and encouraged to develop to their fullest potential. Mission: Provide high quality education and childcare in a safe, respectful and inclusive environment that builds a foundation for life-long learning, globally competative and socially responsible citizens
